Shu Jia's Imaging Lab

GATECH

The lab's research is focused on biophotonics, super-resolution and advanced optical microscopy. The lab are particularly interested in new imaging physics, bottom-up opto-electronic system design, as well as new principles for light propagation, light-matter interaction and image formation in complex biological materials, especially at the single-molecule level.

USTC
