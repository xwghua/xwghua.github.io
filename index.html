News about Me (Updated in Nov.2015)

Currently, I, with my teammates, are operating a project named Simultaneous Three-dimensional Tracking and Fluorescent Imaging of Microorganism Motility. And our first-step work of 3D tracing in cooperation with lab seniors has already been recognized and won the Special Prize in the USTC Students' Physical Experiments Competition in Jan. this year.


Right now, we have managed to take images through both cameras at the same time with LabVIEW, and the transfer of data from LabVIEW towards MATLAB has been working stably. However, the quality of imaging requires improving by rearranging the optical elements and adjusting the path of light propagation.


These days I'm quite busy with application for Ph.D. studies abroad. And I'm looking forward to receiving my offer.

It is close to the end of the year. And let's work hard for the harvest.
